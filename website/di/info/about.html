About Global Encryption Day SF

Welcome.
"Global Encryption Day - San Francisco” is a pivotal gathering orchestrated by the Global Encryption Coalition.

This event shines a spotlight on the crucial role of encryption in safeguarding our digital privacy and security. Join forces with the local chapters of the Internet Society and the Association for Computing Machinery as we delve into the transformative power of encryption. For more insights into the Global Encryption Coalition, the significance of encryption, and to amplify your digital defense, visit https://www.globalencryption.org.

Don’t miss this enlightening opportunity at the 2024 event in San Francisco!

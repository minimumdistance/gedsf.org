Global Encryption Day 2024

October 21, 2024

San Francisco

Join us for Global Encryption Day 2024, co-hosted by the Global Encryption Coalition, the Internet Society - San Francisco Bay Area Chapter, and the Association for Computing Machinery - San Francisco Bay Area Chapter. This crucial event promotes the vital role of encryption in securing privacy and digital communications. Attendance is free, but registration is required two days prior. With two tracks offered at nearby locations, this event welcomes both tech-savvy individuals and those new to computer administration. Register now to explore the transformative power of encryption and strengthen your digital security skills!

About

 "Global Encryption Day - San Francisco" is a local edition of the global Global Encryption Day event ran by the Global Encryption Coalition.

Global Encryption Day (GED) is an annual event organized by the Global Encryption Coalition (GEC), designed to raise awareness about the importance of encryption in protecting privacy and securing digital communications. It brings together various stakeholders, including civil society organizations, technology companies, and individuals, to advocate for strong encryption policies and practices.

To learn more about the Global Encryption Coalition, Global Encryption Day, and more about the shared importance of encryption see https://www.globalencryption.org.

The local professional society chapters of the Internet Society and Association for Computing Machinery have partnered to put on the 2024 event.
